Welcome to My Health

My Health is a comprehensive health management application designed to help you keep track of your medical information and make informed decisions about your health. With My Health, you can:

  • Record and update your personal health details, including height, weight, blood pressure, and cholesterol levels.
  • Track your medical conditions and diseases, making it easier to manage your health.
  • Manage your medications, including dosage instructions and prescriber details.
  • Keep a list of your healthcare providers, making it simple to contact them when needed.
  • Store information about your preferred pharmacies for quick access.
  • Access information about drugs from the OpenFDA API, helping you stay informed about your medications.

Our user-friendly interface and secure data storage make it easy to manage and update your health information from anywhere, ensuring that you always have access to your critical medical details. Join us today and take control of your health with My Health!
